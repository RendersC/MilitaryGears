Frequently Asked Questions

Is Military Store a legal business?
Yes, Military Store operates strictly within local laws and regulations. All items listed comply with legal requirements for civilian or authorized professional purchase.
Can I order restricted items online?
No, restricted or controlled items cannot be shipped directly without proper documentation. Buyers must provide licenses or identification according to regional laws before any transaction.
Do you provide international shipping?
Currently, Military Store serves domestic customers only. Export of regulated equipment requires additional permits and is handled case by case.
How can I contact support?
You can contact us through the form on the Contacts page or send an email to support@militarystore.example.
